Global Village

The Dubai Global Village is a huge amusement park complex like "Mini Israel" of all the countries of the world with 26 pavilions. Each pavilion represents a different country and operates within it cultural and entertainment events, street food stalls and attractions unique to that country. Beyond cultural shows, there are: parades, a museum and other activities. There is no doubt that this is an attraction in Dubai that is highly recommended and attractive for families with children in Dubai and for young people who like to have fun as children.

Dubai Marina

The dubai Marina is a pastoral resort complex with luxury yacht cruises into the sunset, open-air restaurants, designer cafes and temporary handicraft markets nestled along the beach promenade. In the Dubai Marina you can run along the boardwalk, rent bicycles for riding, there is a playground for children, and sandy beaches that allow you to just sit back and relax.
